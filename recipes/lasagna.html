Lasagna

Description

From what I can tell, the secret behind Alton's lasagna is the addition of a little sugar to the sauce and using a bit more cheese than most other recipes.

Ingredients

  • 2 teaspoons of olive oil
  • pound ground beef
  • 1/2 onion
  • 3/8 tomato
  • pinch garlic
  1. Start by making the sauce
  2. Let this simmer while you boil the noodles and get the cheeses ready
  3. from there, it's just an assembly job
  4. Bake until bubbly
